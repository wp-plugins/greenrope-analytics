== Description ==

# GreenRope: CRM, Email Marketing, and more!

## Now for WordPress!

### This plugin is designed to track your WordPress site vistors within your GreenRope account.

Simply install and activate the GreenRope plugin on your WordPress site and enter your account number.  Voila!  The GreenRope tracking code will be dynamically inserted on every page of your WordPress website and the analytics will be available in your GreenRope account.

GreenRope is a powerful, web-based application that allows business owners to consolidate their customer database, create marketing campaigns, send and track invoicing and that’s just the beginning, all while being more efficient and effective than ever before. No matter what kind of small business you are running, we work hard to make your life easier through consolidation while still offering robust features. All your data is stored securely online allowing customers easy access to it from anywhere in the world. All that is needed is an internet connection.

All of the features available are accessible from one screen, making this an easy-to-use, feature-packed solution for small business management.

## To register for a free GreenRope trial account, click here: http://www.greenrope.com/app2/create_account.pl

== Installation ==

### The Easy Way:

1. In your WordPress site admin area, click on "Plugins" and then "Add New".


2. Enter "greenrope" in the search box.


3. Click on the "Install Now" link.

***

### The Manual Way:

1. Download the plugin from this page, http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/greenrope-analytics/


2. Extract the contents of greenrope.zip to your wp-content/plugins/ folder. You'll then see a folder called greenrope.


3. Activate the GreenRope plugin in your WordPress admin area.


4. Click on the GreenRope menu and simply enter your GreenRope account number.  You're all set and your WordPress site analytics will begin to flow to your GreenRope account!

***
** NOTE: Locate your GreenRope account number by logging in to your GreenRope account (http://www.greenrope.com/app2/login.pl) and going to the My Account > Subscription page **

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= 1.  Do I need a GreenRope account to use the plugin? =
Answer: Yes.  You have to have an account to use GreenRope's tracking.

= 2.  Where do I locate my GreenRope Account Number? =
Locate your GreenRope account number by logging in to your GreenRope account (http://www.greenrope.com/app2/login.pl) and going to the My Account > Subscription page.

= 3.  How do I track individual contacts? =
Answer: Be sure you import your contacts in to your GreenRope account and tracking will happen automatically

= 4.  Does GreenRope offer a trial account? =
Answer: Yes.  You can use your GreenRope account for 30 days to try it out.


For more information, please visit http://itegritygroup.com/wordpress/plugins/greenrope
